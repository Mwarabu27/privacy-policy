# Privacy Policy for EduSuccess

**Last Updated:** [Insert Date]

## Introduction

Welcome to EduSuccess ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

By using EduSuccess, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We may collect the following types of personal information:

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, username, and password
- **Profile Information:** Grade level, school name (optional), profile picture (optional)
- **Academic Data:** Grades, assignments, study schedules, and academic progress
- **Usage Information:** How you interact with the app, features used, and time spent

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Log Data:** App usage statistics, crash reports, and performance data
- **Location Data:** General location (city/region) for localized content (with permission)

### Information from Third Parties
- **Social Media:** If you choose to connect social media accounts (profile information only)
- **Educational Platforms:** Data imported from connected educational services (with your consent)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### Primary Functions
- **Academic Tracking:** Monitor and display your academic progress and achievements
- **Personalization:** Customize the app experience based on your preferences and usage
- **Notifications:** Send reminders about assignments, study sessions, and achievements
- **Support:** Provide customer service and technical assistance

### App Improvement
- **Analytics:** Understand how users interact with the app to improve functionality
- **Bug Fixes:** Identify and resolve technical issues
- **Feature Development:** Develop new features based on user needs

### Communication
- **Updates:** Inform you about app updates and new features
- **Educational Content:** Share study tips and academic resources

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do NOT Sell Your Information
We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for commercial purposes.

### Limited Sharing Scenarios
We may share your information only in these specific circumstances:

- **With Your Consent:** When you explicitly authorize us to share information
- **Service Providers:** With trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the app (under strict confidentiality agreements)
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law, court order, or government authorities
- **Safety Concerns:** To protect the rights, property, or safety of users or others
- **Business Transfers:** In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified)

### Educational Institution Sharing
- **School Integration:** If you choose to connect with your school's systems, we may share relevant academic data with authorized school personnel
- **Parent/Guardian Access:** For users under 18, parents/guardians may request access to their child's data

## Data Security

### Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Secure Servers:** Data is stored on secure, regularly monitored servers
- **Access Controls:** Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
- **Regular Audits:** Periodic security assessments and updates

### Data Breach Response
In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:
- Notify affected users within 72 hours
- Report to relevant authorities as required by law
- Take immediate steps to secure the breach
- Provide updates on remediation efforts

## Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Control
You have the right to:

- **View Your Data:** Request a copy of all personal information we have about you
- **Update Information:** Modify or correct your personal information
- **Delete Account:** Request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Data Portability:** Export your data in a commonly used format
- **Withdraw Consent:** Revoke consent for data processing (where applicable)

### Parental Rights (Users Under 18)
Parents and legal guardians have the right to:
- Review their child's personal information
- Request deletion of their child's account
- Refuse further collection of their child's information

### How to Exercise Your Rights
To exercise any of these rights, contact us at:
- **Email:** privacy@edusuccess.com
- **In-App:** Use the "Privacy Settings" section
- **Response Time:** We will respond within 30 days

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

### Special Protections for Minors
We take extra precautions to protect children's privacy:

- **Parental Consent:** We obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting information from users under 13
- **Limited Collection:** We collect only information necessary for the app's educational functions
- **No Behavioral Advertising:** We do not use children's information for targeted advertising
- **Educational Purpose:** All data collection serves legitimate educational interests

### Age Verification
- Users must verify their age during account creation
- Users under 13 require parental consent to create an account
- Users 13-17 can create accounts but parents are notified

## Data Retention

### Retention Periods
- **Active Accounts:** Data is retained while your account is active
- **Inactive Accounts:** Data is deleted after 2 years of inactivity
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Legal Requirements:** Some data may be retained longer if required by law

### Data Deletion Process
When you delete your account:
1. Your profile and personal information are immediately removed
2. Academic data is anonymized and may be retained for analytics
3. Backup systems are purged within 30 days
4. You will receive confirmation of deletion
